# Product Overview

## What is this?
A professional portfolio and landing page for Natalia Rainyk, a psychodynamic psychotherapist based in Poland.

## Purpose
- Showcase therapy services and professional credentials
- Provide pricing and location information
- Enable potential clients to make contact/book appointments
- Build trust through professional, calming design

## Target Audience
Polish-speaking individuals seeking psychotherapy services, particularly those interested in psychodynamic therapy approaches.

## Key Features
- Single-page scrolling design with anchor navigation
- Sections: Hero, About, Services, For Who, How I Work, Pricing, Location, Contact
- Contact form for appointment requests
- Mobile-responsive design
- Scroll-triggered animations

## Language
All user-facing content is in Polish (pl).
